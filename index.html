Rush Hour 2.0

This visualization demonstrates how adjusting train times can reduce the overall time Atlantans spend waiting for trains.

Adjust the slider to see the effect on the randomly-generated trips below, or press 'Optimize' to find the ideal solution for the current dataset.

This system could be applied to large yearly datasets or aggregated datasets (ex: all Falcons gamedays) to predict how alternate and potentially dynamic schedules could improve Atlanta commute times without the cost of adding new trains.
